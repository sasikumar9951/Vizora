About the Event

What Is VIZORA?

A premier 2-day UI&UX design competition by Dept. of CSE, Mahendra Engineering College — built to challenge and inspire the next generation of digital creators.

◈

What is UI/UX?

UI (User Interface) is the visual layer — buttons, colors, typography. UX (User Experience) is the complete journey a user takes. Together, they define how people feel when they interact with digital products.

⬡

What Will You Do?

Design a full mobile app UI/UX solution for a real-world problem. From wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes in Figma. Present your working prototype to a panel of industry experts.

✦

Why Participate?

Win cash prizes up to ₹2000, gain industry exposure, and add a prestigious event to your portfolio. VIZORA opens doors to internships and design careers at top tech companies.
